  • A $200 security deposit (or credit card pre-authorisation) is required per apartment booked, as per our Terms and Conditions.

  • All apartments are strictly NO SMOKING.

  • All prices are in Australian Dollars ($AUD) and include GST.

  • All bookings are subject to our Terms and Conditions.

  • We look after our regular customers. If you stay with us often, we will give you discounts, especially when you stay multiple nights off-peak.

  • We also give discounts to people who stay multiple nights at off-peak times.

  • For peak periods, minimum booking lengths apply. Examples: for Easter there is normally a 3 night minimum, and for the Christmas - New Year period, there is normally a 5 night minimum.   • A cot for baby can be arranged for an additional $20 AUD per night. Please make sure you arrange this with us before your date of arrival.


Pet Friendly Apartments:

  • We do have 3 (three) specially designated "pet friendly" / "dog friendly" apartments. Clean, well behaved adult dogs only. Pets must be supervised at all times, and cannot be left in the apartment on their own.

  • Note that pets are only allowed in our specially designated "pet friendly" apartments.

  • If you are travelling with your pet, then please make sure you book one of our specially designated "Pet Friendly" apartments and let us know how many pets you are traveling with. ' Our "pet friendly" apartments are very popular ! Please book in advance to avoid disappointment.

  • An additional $10 AUD per pet per night.

  • We can provide pet towels, pet bowls, and pet bedding if required.

  • Three (3) of our apartments are designated PET FRIENDLY apartments. Please click here for further information about our PET FRIENDLY apartments. Or, click here for pictures of one of our "Pet Friendly" apartments.


Weekly Rates / Long Stay Discounts:

  • Special discounts are available for people who stay for a week or longer during off-peak times. If you are staying multiple nights, then we do provide special deals and prices, especially during quieter off-peak times ! The longer you stay, the better deal we can do.

  • Example: pay for 6 nights and get the 7th night *FREE* !!


Repeat Visitors / Regular Customers:

  • We love our regulars - people who stay with us frequently, or for weeks or months every year. We really look after our regulars and provide special deals and prices for Repeat Visitors / Regular Customers, especially during quieter off-peak times.
