Links and Local Attractions:

    Here are some of the local attractions and "things to do" in our region.

    Note: In addition to the list below, we have prepared a booklet of 90+ activities and "things to do" in our region, along with contact details, web links, phone numbers, suitable weather conditions, prices, etc, and this booklet is inside all of our apartments and also on the check-in desk at reception. We also have many brochures and further information available in reception, and all of our staff are long term residents in the region so they are very knowledgeable about our area. If there is anything we don't know, or if you have any questions we cannot answer, then we will find out for you !

  • Capricorn Coast Tourist Office - Visit the Capricorn Coast - exciting, diverse and breathtakingly beautiful. Great photo gallery and lots of useful visitor information and links to local attractions.

  • Keppel Bay Marina - Boat Cruises, Waterfront Residences, Used Boats, Charter, Chandlery, Boatyard, Waterfront Cafe.

  • Keppel Bay Sailing Club - awesome ~$12 carvery buffet, poker machines, great sea and island views.

  • Emu Park On-Line - a promotional web site for the best kept secret on the east coast. Great photo gallery and lots of useful information.

  • Koorana Saltwater Crocodile Farm - see large crocs, see baby crocs hatch, hold a small croc, buy croc leather belts, shoes, etc of Australia.

  • Cooberrie Park - cuddle a Koala, hold a Snake, and hand feed our very friendly Kangaroos, and see more than 300 native Australian animals.

  • Capricorn Caves - a spectacular and unique system of above-ground caves in a limestone ridge situated 23km north of Rockhampton, Central Queensland.

  • Capricorn Reef Diving - offer a range of courses from Introductory (Resort) Scuba Experiences and Open Water Diver Courses through to our Instructor programmes.

  • Causeway Lake Boat Hire - explore and fish the magnificent Causeway Lake tidal estuary system, just a few KMs from Kinka Palms Motel.

  • Footlights Theatre Restaurant - offers the finest in Entertainment, Food and Atmosphere, with their Top Class Comedy Variety Show and Delicious Three or Four Course Dinner.

  • Great Western Hotel - Rockhampton's premier entertainment venue and action packed undercover rodeo & concert arena. Escape the rush of the city life and relax in the truly Australian atmosphere of The Great Western Hotel.

  • Nob Creek Pottery - offers outstanding Australian ceramic art for your home.

  • Shell World - be prepared to be amazed at the outstanding display of shells - it will delight every age group.

  • Rubyvale Gem Gallery - on display in our gallery are sapphires mined from one of the world's largest sapphire fields located on the Tropic of Capricorn in Central Queensland, Australia.

  • Mount Morgan Town, Mine & Cave Tours - A complete, unique, guided tour viewing the present and discovering the past of Mount Morgan township, old gold mine and Jurassic Caves.

  • Yeppoon Info - photo panoramas, accommodation, events, schools, travel articles, and more for Yeppoon, Byfield, Causway Lake, Kemp Beach, Mulambin, Lammermoor, Kinka Beach, Emu Park, Keppel Islands, Lake Mary, Woodbury, Bungundarra, and surrounding areas of the Capricorn Coast.

  • Heli-Central (Rosslyn Bay Marina, Yeppoon) provide scenic helicopter rides around the coast, Five Rocks, Keppel Islands, etc. Ph. Shane on 0413 275 773, or see HeliCentral on Facebook.
