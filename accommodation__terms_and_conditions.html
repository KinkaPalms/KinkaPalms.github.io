Terms and Conditions

v1.02
  • By checking-in to our motel, you confirm your acceptance of our Terms and Conditions and our Cancellation Policy.
  • All prices are in Australian Dollars (AUD) and include GST.

Considerate, Quiet, Peaceful Enjoyment:

  • We want all of our motel guests to have a quiet, peaceful, enjoyable stay at our motel, and a great night's sleep.
  • Guests are expected to be considerate and courteous to other guests and motel staff.
  • Guests are expected to treat our motel apartments and facilities with respect.

NO Credit:

  • No credit under any circumstances.
  • All accommodation costs, fees, charges to be paid in advance and certainly paid in full before departing the motel.

Security Deposit: minimum $200.00

  • Security Deposit: We require a minimum of $200.00 security deposit per apartment prior to check-in.
  • A higher Security Deposit may be required if we feel that it is appropriate.
  • This Security Deposit can be paid by pre-authorisation of a valid credit card, or via any of our Accepted Payment Methods.
  • If it looks like you Security Deposit is not going to be enough to cover any costs, fees, or charges you incur, then we will (1). debit your credit card to cover any amounts owing, and/or (2). place additional pre-authorisations against your credit card to cover any amounts owing and/or (3). request further immediate payments from you if a credit card is not available, to ensure we are fully protected.
  • If your Security Deposit is not enough to cover any amounts owing, and/or your credit card cannot be debited to cover any amounts owing, then you must settle your bill in full via some other payment means before departing the motel.
  • If this isn't possible, consult with Motel staff and make arrangements to leave acceptable collateral while you organise payments for whatever amounts are owing.
  • At our discretion, we may wave the requirement for a Security Deposit for our regular customers.

Responsibility / Supervision / Valuables:

  • Motel / motel staff are not responsible for any damage or loss.
  • Motel customers are responsible for their children, pets, visitors.
  • No valuables or belongings to be left unattended.
  • Children must be supervised by their parents / guardians at all times.
  • Pets must be supervised at all times. Pets are not to be left at the motel unsupervised at any time. Pets are only allowed in our designated "pet friendly" apartments.

Additional Beds / People:

  • Additional bed usage: $20.00 per bed per night
  • Additional people staying in rooms: $20.00 per person per night

Apartment Servicing:

  • Off Peak: We will give your apartment a basic service - empty bins, etc - every morning if you vacate your apartment between 8 AM and 11 AM.
  • Peak Times (Christmas, Easter, etc): We don't want to disturb people during peak times. If you need new bin bags, etc please let us know.
  • Bed sheets are changed every 7 days.
  • Towels are changed every 3-4 days.
  • If you require more frequent changes, contact motel staff, additional charges apply.
  • We perform a full apartment service - change sheets, towels, vacuum carpets, mop kitchen and bathroom floors, clean toilet, shower, etc - after every check-out.

Cleaning and Damage:

  • If you do have an "accident" in the apartment, please notify motel staff as soon as possible (during office hours) to reduce cleaning time and expense.
  • People are expected to keep their apartments in a clean state throughout their stay and leave the apartment in a clean state when they check-out, including all used cutlery, crockery, pots, pans, etc washed, wiped, and put away, kitchen benches wiped, spillages in fridge, etc wiped up, toasters to be cleaned and shaken out before being put back in the cupboard, etc. If this isn't the case, then cleaning fees will be charged to your account. Minimum charge: $30.00.
  • Towel, bath linen abuse: hair dye, make-up, grease, etc on our white towels is charged at $25.00 per item.
  • Additional charges apply if an apartment is damaged, or bedding is soiled, or left unclean. Minimim charge: $30.00.
  • Lost or unreturned Room Key charge: $60.00.
  • Lost or unreturned Wardrobe Key charge: $10.00.
  • If cleaning and/or repairs cannot by 2:00 PM that day, then the cost of the $Room Rate per day (without discounts) will be applied to your account for as long as it takes to complete the cleaning or the repairs. Minimim charge: $Room Rate per day (without discounts).
  • NO Pets: A minimum charge of $200.00 per apartment applies if a pet enters a "no pets allowed" apartment. (Pets are only allowed in our designated "pet friendly" apartments.)
  • NO Smoking: all of our apartments are strictly NO SMOKING. A minimum charge of $200.00 per apartment applies for smoking inside any of our apartment.

Electrical Applicances:

  • All prices assume that people will:
    1. Only operate air conditioners at the setting of 20 degrees or higher.
    2. Only operate air conditioners / heaters with all exterior doors and windows closed.
    3. Turn off all air conditioners / heaters, ceiling fans, TVs, if you leave the apartment.
  • If you operate an air conditioner / heater with any exterior doors / windows open for a significant period of time, then it will cut out and you will have to ask motel staff to re-set your air conditioner. We only do this resetting during office hours.
  • If you do not want to abide by these requirements - i.e. you want to waste electricity - then please let us know so that your nightly rate can be adjusted / extra fees applied.

Banned Items and Activities:

The following are BANNED from motel apartments and grounds:
  1. Illegal drugs, drug paraphanalia, and all other illegal items.
  2. Any dangerous or illegal items: guns, bows, sling shots, crackers, fire works, rockets, explosives, etc.
  3. Chewing gum
  4. Glitter
  5. Hair Dye
  6. Illegal and/or unsavory activities.
  7. Running businesses or business activities.
  8. Doing anything that may endanger motel staff or motel property, or endanger other guests or their property.
  9. Doing anything that could bring the motel into disrepute.

Visitors:

  • Motel customers are responsible for their children, pets, visitors.
  • Our facilities (pool, BBQ, sun lounges, tables chairs, etc) are provided for our motel customers.
  • Visitors are allowed to visit motel customers, but they should park at the front of the motel (between the road and the pool).
  • Vistors are not allowed to use the pool.
  • No visitors are allowed after 8:00 PM.
  • Visitors are not allowed to bring pets onto motel property.
  • Any visitors present at the motel after 8:00 PM at night will either need to check-in and pay for suitable accommodation (if available), or else leave the motel.

Excessive Noise (after 10 PM):

To ensure all motel guests have a quiet, pleasant stay, please keep noise to a minimum after 10 PM.
  • Excessive noise (after 10 PM) – 1st warning incurs no charge.
  • Charge is $50.00 per warning (after 1st warning).
  • More than 3 warnings will result in immediate eviction.
  • If your excessive noise or bad behaviour results in other motel guests checking-out early, then you will be paying us for the cost of their cancelled nights.

Evictions:

You will be evicted for any of the following reasons:
  • Swearing, loud noise, anti-social behaviour, and/or bad behaviour by you or your visitors.
  • Fighting with, swearing at, arguing with, and/or abusing other guests or motel staff by you or your visitors.
  • Having / doing any of the Banned Items / Activities, and failing to immediately remove / stop them when asked by motel staff.
  • Disabling, resetting, tampering with air conditioner reset switches.
  • More than a total of 3 warnings about Excessive Noise (after 10 PM) during your stay.
There are NO REFUNDS if you are evicted. In addition, you will lose your Security Deposit.
