Cancellation Policy: (Last Updated: 27-Oct-2019)

  • Please understand that the last thing we want to do is upset anyone or discourage guests from booking or returning to our motel because we have kept their deposit or charged a cancellation fee following a cancellation, however, we must protect ourselves from the financial loss and extra work caused by cancellations.

  • We recommend that all travelers take out suitable Travel Insurance (see below for further details).

  • The Cancellation Policy that applies to your booking depends on when the booking is for.

  • Changes made to a booking on the day of arrival or after arrival, such as shortened bookings and changed nights, will be treated as cancellations and will be subject to the cancellation fees below.

  • "No shows", where the guest fails to check-in on the booked arrival date, will be treated as cancellations and will be subject to the cancellation fees below.


Cancellation Policy for Off-Peak Periods:

  • If you cancel on the day of arrival or during your stay, cancellation fee = cost of 1 night of accommodation (for each apartment booked).


Cancellation Policy for Mid-Season (school holidays, long weekends, public holidays, etc):

  • If you cancel within 14 days of the day of arrival or during your stay, cancellation fee = cost of your booked nights or 2 nights of accommodation (for each apartment booked), whichever is the lesser.

  • Example 1: If you booked an apartment for 2 nights and you cancel within 14 days of arrival, then the cancellation fee is 2 nights of accommodation (for each apartment booked).

  • Example 2: If you booked an apartment for 1 night and you cancel within 14 days of arrival, then the cancellation fee is 1 night of accommodation (for each apartment booked).


Cancellation Policy for Peak Periods (Easter, Christmas, New Year, Surf Carnivals, Weddings, Events, Shows, OctoberFest, etc):

  • If you cancel within 90 days of the day of arrival or during your stay, cancellation fee = cost of your booked nights or 5 nights of accommodation (for each apartment booked), whichever is the lesser.

  • Example 1: If you booked an apartment for 2 nights and you cancel within 90 days of arrival, then the cancellation fee is 2 nights of accommodation (for each apartment booked).

  • Example 2: If you booked an apartment for 20 nights and you cancel within 90 days of arrival, then the cancellation fee is 5 nights of accommodation (for each apartment booked).


Travel Insurance:

    If there is any chance that you may need to cancel or change your booking because of accident, illness, injury, family emergency, fire, bad weather, flood, travel delays, changes of plan, employment issues, health issues, car breakdowns, flight delays, flight cancellations, or any other reasons, then we highly recommend that you take out suitable Travel Insurance. A good place to start is an internet search (e.g. search on: "cancellation insurance" +Australia ). This search will list numerous insurance companies that may be able to help. You need to do your own research and verifications before taking out any insurance, to make sure the insurance covers exactly what you need it to cover.

    We highly recommend travel and cancellation insurance and urge everyone to take out suitable travel and cancellation insurance when traveling at any time, and especially when this travel is at peak times. Such insurance is usually available at very reasonable prices. For example, it costs approximately the same as 1 night's accommodation for 4 weeks of Travel and Cancellation Insurance at peak times.
