Meals, Restaurants, Take-Aways, Supermarkets, etc:

  • We don't have a restaurant on site .... well, not yet anyway !!

  • Kitchen: However, every single one of our apartments is fully self-contained with its own kitchen-ette (sink, microwave, bench top hotplate, toaster, full sized fridge / freezer, cutlery, crockery, and cooking pots and pans and microwave dishes, etc). So, you can prepare your own meals if you like.

  • Supermarkets: Large supermarkets (Coles, Woollies, Big W, etc) are located at Rockhampton (45 Km to the West) and Yeppoon (12 Km to the North). A Super IGA supermarket is located at Emu Park (5 KM to the South).

  • Corner stores: where you can buy drinks and some basic food items, like bread, milk, drinks, etc are located nearby. The nearest is located 3 doors from our motel.

  • Restaurants: Numerous restaurants (such as Thai, Chinese, Italian, Indian, sea food, steak, etc), as well as up-market resorts and the Keppel Bay Sailing Club , are all within a 12 Km radius. The nearest restaurant, Kinka Kippa, is 0.5 Km from our motel.

  • Take Aways: Numerous take-aways (such as Thai, Chinese, Italian, fish and chips, hamburgers, etc) are all within a 12 Km radius. The nearest take-away, Kinka Kippa, is 0.5 Km from our motel, and sells fish and chips, hamburgers, etc.
