Apartment / Room Configurations:

    We have 13 apartments, which can accommodate up to 38 people per night.

    All of our apartments are fully self-contained, air-conditioned, ground level, beach front, studio apartments, each with a large screen LCD TV, DVD player, kitchen-ette, bathroom, and all linen and bath towels are supplied, free wireless internet access, in a lovely, spacious tropical garden setting with sparkling in-ground pool and located just across the road from beautiful Kinka Beach. The kitchen-ette inside each apartment includes cutlery, crockery, a few pots and pans, a microwave dish, toaster, kettle, a twin hot-plate (plug-in), and a full size fridge / freezer. Kinka Beach has stunning views of Keppel Bay and more than a dozen islands, including Great Keppel Island (about 15 Km offshore).

    Our family apartments cater for up to 5 people and feature a separate bedroom for added space and privacy.

    The apartment / room configurations and the number of each type of apartment are as follows:

    • 7 x "Double Apartment-S2": Studio apartment with queen sized bed in main room (with TV, kitchen, etc).
      Please click here for photos / plan of our Double Apartment S2 apartment.

    • 1 x "Twin Apartment-S3": Studio apartment with double bed + single bed in the main room (with TV, kitchen, etc).
      Please click here for photos / plan of our Twin Apartment S3 apartment.

    • 4 x "Family Apartment-S4": Studio apartment with 2 Rooms - Queen sized bed in main room (with TV, kitchen, etc), sliding door, and 2 x single beds in a separate bedroom (or vice versa).
      Please click here for photos / plan of our Family Apartment S4 apartment.

    • 1 x "Family Apartment-S5": Studio apartment with 2 Rooms - 1 x double bed and 1 x single bed in one separate bedroom, and 2 x single beds in main room (with TV, kitchen, etc).
      NOTE: This apartment is probably phased out in the very near future.
      Please click here for photos / plan of our Family Apartment S5 apartment.

    All apartments are air-cond, ground level, and all linen and towels supplied.

    All apartments are strictly NO SMOKING.
